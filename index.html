Foundation Models For Medical ImagING (FOMMIA) - UCL KnowLab Section

MICCAI2024 Tutorial

SUNDAY, OCTOBER 6TH 2024 11:10-12:10

Diamant Room at Palmeraie Palace

This tutorial section aims to explore the applications of Large Vision Language Models (LVLMs) in the context of medical image analytics.

The tutorial section is designed to provide the audience with an introduction to LVLMs especially within the healthcare domain. With a strong emphasis on practical applications, the objectives extend to demonstrate comprehensive skills to proficiently apply and refine these models.

Tutorial Presenters

Yunsoo Kim

Yunsoo Kim is a PhD candidate at the Institute of Health Informatics (IHI), University College London (UCL), where he is actively involved in research related to medical multimodal large language models. Before his PhD, he was a senior research scientist and worked as the NLP group leader at LG Chem.

Jinge Wu

Jinge is a PhD candidate at IHI, UCL, where she is experienced in medical multimodal language models for various clinical applications. Jinge has presented her work at international conferences and collaborates with healthcare professionals to apply her research in practical settings.

Honghan Wu

Professor Honghan Wu is a Professor of Health Informatics and AI at the University of Glasgow, and currently hold an honorary Associate Professor position with the IHI, UCL where he leads a Health Informatics Group called KnowLab. His research interest is the intersection of health informatics and multimodal large language models.
